## Accessing Your Profile

Navigate to **Mi Perfil** from the sidebar or go directly to `/dashboard/teacher/profile`. The page fetches your current data from:

```http
GET /coordinadores/{userId}
```

The response contains a nested structure where personal fields live in the `user` object and coordinator-specific fields live in `user.coordinadores`:

```json
{
  "data": {
    "id": 7,
    "nombres": "Carlos",
    "apellidos": "Mamani",
    "email": "carlos.mamani@gema.pe",
    "telefono_personal": "987654321",
    "coordinadores": {
      "especializacion": "Voleibol Técnico"
    }
  }
}
```

## Profile Fields

Your profile page displays two sections:

### Información Personal

| Field | Database column | Description |
|-------|----------------|-------------|
| **Email Asociado** | `usuarios.email` | Your login email address |
| **Celular / WhatsApp** | `usuarios.telefono_personal` | Your contact phone number |

### Datos Profesionales

| Field | Database column | Description |
|-------|----------------|-------------|
| **Especialización** | `profesores.especializacion` | Your volleyball or training specialization (e.g., "Voleibol Técnico") |

<Info>
  Your full name (`nombres` + `apellidos`) is displayed in the hero card at the top of the profile page and in the top-right corner of every page header. It also appears on class session cards visible to enrolled students.
</Info>

## Editing Your Profile

Click **Editar Datos** in the hero card to open the edit modal.

### Editable Fields

The modal is divided into two sections:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Datos Generales">
    | Field | Form key | Notes |
    |-------|----------|-------|
    | Nombres | `nombres` | Your first name(s) |
    | Apellidos | `apellidos` | Your last name(s) |
    | Email Asociado | `email` | Must be a valid email address |
    | Celular / WhatsApp | `telefono_personal` | Freeform text |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Datos Profesionales">
    | Field | Form key | Notes |
    |-------|----------|-------|
    | Especialización | `especializacion` | Freeform text (e.g., "Voleibol Técnico", "Baloncesto Técnico") |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### How Updates Are Sent

Clicking **GUARDAR CAMBIOS** sends a `PATCH` request:

```http
PATCH /coordinadores/{userId}
```

The payload only includes fields that have a non-empty value — blank fields are omitted:

```json
{
  "nombres": "Carlos",
  "apellidos": "Mamani",
  "email": "carlos.mamani@gema.pe",
  "telefono_personal": "987654321",
  "especializacion": "Voleibol Técnico"
}
```

On success:
- A toast notification confirms `"¡Datos actualizados!"`
- The modal closes
- The profile page re-fetches your data from `/coordinadores/{userId}` to reflect the changes immediately
- The auth context is also updated so your name in the header and class cards refreshes without a page reload

<Warning>
  If you leave **Nombres** or **Apellidos** blank in the form, those fields are omitted from the payload and your existing name is preserved. To intentionally clear a field, you must contact an administrator.
</Warning>

## Changing Your Password

Click **Contraseña** in the hero card to open the password change modal. This is handled by the shared `ChangePasswordModal` component.

The endpoint used is:

```http
POST /auth/change-password
```

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the modal">
    Click **Contraseña** on your profile page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your current password">
    Type your existing password in the first field to verify your identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a new password">
    Enter your new password and confirm it in the second and third fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Submit the form. On success you receive a confirmation toast and the modal closes. Your next login will require the new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Password changes take effect immediately. If you are logged in on other devices or browsers, those sessions may be invalidated depending on your token refresh settings.
</Note>

## Profile Impact on Other Views

Your profile data is not isolated to this page — several other parts of the platform read from it:

| Where your data appears | Which field |
|-------------------------|-------------|
| Dashboard greeting (`HOLA, NOMBRE 👋`) | `nombres` + `apellidos` |
| Top-right header badge | `nombres` + `apellidos`, `rol` |
| Sidebar branding | Your initial (`nombres.charAt(0)`) |
| Hero card avatar | Your initial (`nombres.charAt(0)`) |

Keeping your `nombres` and `apellidos` accurate ensures every student and admin who sees a class card attributed to you sees the correct instructor name.
