## What is a Coordinador?

A **Coordinador** (coordinator) is the instructor who leads volleyball training sessions at Academia Gema. Your role centers on three core responsibilities:

- **Taking attendance** â€” recording each athlete's presence or absence at every scheduled session
- **Monitoring billing cycles** â€” reviewing the cut day (`DĂ­a de Corte`) report to identify students with overdue payments
- **Maintaining your profile** â€” keeping your contact details and specialization up to date

In the database, coordinators are stored in the `profesores` table (linked to `usuarios` via `usuario_id`) and have a dedicated `especializacion` field. Your name appears on every class card shown to enrolled students.

## Dashboard Layout

The coordinator interface uses the `TeacherLayout`, which is intentionally minimal compared to the admin or student layouts. It consists of:

| Region | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Left sidebar** (desktop) | Fixed 256 px navigation panel with the Gema Coordinator branding |
| **Top header** (desktop) | Displays your full name and role badge (`Coordinador`) |
| **Main content area** | Light slate background (`#f1f5f9`) where each page loads |
| **Mobile bottom navbar** | `MobileNavbarTeacher` bar for handheld devices |

All coordinator routes are prefixed with `/dashboard/teacher/`.

## Quick-Access Summary

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your agenda">
    Go to **Mis Clases** (`/dashboard/teacher`). The page loads every session assigned to you â€” past, present, and future â€” sorted chronologically. Today's session is highlighted in orange and auto-scrolled into view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take attendance">
    Click **TOMAR ASISTENCIA** on the current session's card to open the attendance modal. Mark each athlete and save the entire group in one action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the cut day report">
    Navigate to **DĂ­as de Corte** to see which students' 30-day billing cycle has expired. Pass this information to the administration team so they can follow up on payments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep your profile current">
    Visit **Mi Perfil** to update your `especializacion`, phone number, or email. Your display name is pulled directly from your profile and shown on class cards.
  </Step>
</Steps>
