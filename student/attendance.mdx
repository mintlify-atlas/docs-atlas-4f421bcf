Attendance is recorded by your coordinator at each class session. You cannot mark your own attendance — only coordinators and administrators can do so.

## Where to view your attendance

Your attendance records appear in two places:

- **Dashboard home** (`/dashboard/student`) — the Schedule grid in the main area shows all your attendance records for the selected month and year. Toggle the month filter and cycle selector to browse historical data.
- **Stats bar** — the two cards above the schedule grid show summary statistics fetched from `GET /asistencias/alumno/:id/estadisticas`.

## Attendance statuses

Each attendance record in `registros_asistencia` has an `estado` field with one of four values:

| Estado | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `PRESENTE` | You attended the session |
| `AUSENTE` | You were absent without justification |
| `TARDE` | You arrived late |
| `JUSTIFICADO` | Your absence was officially excused |

## Attendance statistics

The stats bar on the dashboard home shows two metrics derived from `GET /asistencias/alumno/:id/estadisticas`:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Asistencia Real" icon="calendar-check">
    Your attendance percentage. The value is `porcentaje_asistencia_real` from the statistics endpoint. Displayed in **red** when below 50%.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Faltas Totales" icon="history">
    The total number of missed sessions (`detalle.FALTA.cantidad`). These are the sessions eligible for makeup through the Recovery system.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How attendance affects your account

<Accordion title="Recovery eligibility">
  Absences (`AUSENTE`) generate recovery tickets that you can use to schedule makeup sessions. See the [Recoveries](/student/recoveries) guide for how to use them.

  The number of normal recovery sessions you can use per cycle is limited based on your weekly training frequency:
  - Training 2–3 times per week → up to 2 makeup sessions
  - Training 4 or more times per week → up to 4 makeup sessions

  Absences caused by an approved injury have no recovery limit.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Standing and enrollment">
  Your attendance percentage does not directly block enrollment. However, outstanding debts (which can arise from repeated absences) do block new enrollment. Maintaining a good attendance record helps you stay in good standing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Justified absences">
  A `JUSTIFICADO` status is set by a coordinator or administrator. If you believe an absence was due to a valid reason (illness, injury, academy-cancelled session), contact your coordinator to request justification.
</Accordion>

## Filtering your schedule on the dashboard

In the schedule grid on the dashboard home, use the two filter controls to narrow your view:

- **Month filter** — select a specific month or choose **TODOS LOS MESES** to see all records.
- **Cycle selector** — shows the current year by default. The selected year is displayed as **Ciclo YYYY**.

Recovery sessions that appear in your attendance history are marked with `isRecuperacion: true` and are merged with your regular attendance records in the same grid.
