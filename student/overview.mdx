When you log in as a student (Alumno), you land at `/dashboard/student`. This is your central hub for everything related to your membership at Club Gema.

## What's on the dashboard

The home screen loads four sets of data in parallel and displays them in one view:

| Section | What you see |
|---|---|
| **Header** | Your first name, a notification bell with an unread count, and your avatar initial |
| **Benefits carousel** | Rotating cards for Recovery, Referrals, and Injury management — auto-advances every 6 seconds |
| **Stats bar** | Your real attendance percentage and total missed sessions for the current cycle |
| **Weekly timeline** | A visual block-schedule of your classes for the current week |
| **Schedule grid** | Filterable list of all your attendance records (filter by month and year) |
| **Payments panel** | Outstanding debts and recent payment reports side by side |

### Attendance stats

The two stat cards at the top of the main area pull from `GET /asistencias/alumno/:id/estadisticas`:

- **Asistencia Real** — your attendance percentage. Displays in red when below 50%.
- **Faltas Totales** — total missed sessions in the current cycle.

### Notifications

The bell icon in the header shows a live unread count, polled every 5 minutes from `GET /notificaciones/conteo-no-leidas`. Clicking the bell opens a dropdown that splits notifications into **Nuevas** (unread) and **Anteriores** (read). Clicking an unread notification marks it as read via `PATCH /notificaciones/:id/leer`.

### Desktop navigation

On screens wider than `md` (768 px), the sidebar is visible on the left. Each menu item links to a sub-route under `/dashboard/student/`.

### Mobile navigation

On mobile, a bottom navigation bar replaces the sidebar. The same eight sections are accessible as icon buttons along the bottom of the screen.

## Route map

```text
/dashboard/student                   → Home / overview
/dashboard/student/myRegistrations   → My Registrations
/dashboard/student/enrollment        → Enrollment
/dashboard/student/payments          → Payments
/dashboard/student/profile           → Profile
/dashboard/student/injuries          → Injuries
/dashboard/student/recoveries        → Recoveries
/dashboard/student/news              → News (Muro Gema)
```

<Note>
  All routes under `/dashboard/student` require an active session with the **Alumno** role. If your session expires, you are redirected to `/login`.
</Note>
