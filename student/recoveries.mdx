---
title: "Recovery (Makeup) Sessions"
description: "How to schedule makeup classes for sessions you missed, manage your recovery tickets, and understand the recovery limits at Club Gema."
---

Club Gema's recovery system lets you make up classes you missed. Each absence generates a **recovery ticket** (a pending slot). You use a ticket to book yourself into a future class session as a makeup.

## Recovery limits

There are two types of recovery tickets:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Recuperaciones Normales" icon="activity">
    Limited per billing cycle. The limit depends on your training frequency:
    - **2–3 sessions/week** → up to 2 makeups
    - **4+ sessions/week** → up to 4 makeups

    When `recuperacion_usadas` reaches `limite_permitido`, a **Límite Alcanzado** badge appears.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recuperaciones por Lesión" icon="shield-plus">
    Unlimited. Available when you have an approved injury request. There is no cap on how many makeup sessions you can schedule under this type.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Recovery benefits are suspended if you have an outstanding debt with status `PENDIENTE` or `PARCIAL`. Clear your balance first.
</Warning>

## Accessing the Recovery section

Go to `/dashboard/student/recoveries`. The page loads three data sets in parallel:

- **Pending tickets** from `GET /recuperaciones/pendientes` — absences eligible for a makeup.
- **Available schedules** from the schedule service — all active class slots.
- **Recovery history** from `GET /recuperaciones/historial` — all past and upcoming makeup sessions.

The stats cards at the top show your current normal recovery usage vs. your limit, and confirm that injury recoveries have no limit.

## Scheduling a makeup session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Agendar Clase tab">
    The **Agendar Clase** tab is the default view. It shows the two-step booking flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a pending absence (ticket)">
    In the first section, you see your pending recovery tickets — one card per absence you have not yet scheduled a makeup for. Click a ticket to select it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an available slot">
    After selecting a ticket, a grid of available class slots appears for the next four weeks. Slots that overlap with your regular schedule or that you already have a makeup booked for are automatically excluded.

    <Note>
      Slots that fall within your current billing cycle on the same schedule as your regular classes are protected and not shown as available, to avoid double-booking your regular spot.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the booking">
    Click a slot. A confirmation toast appears showing the date and start time. Click **Confirmar** to proceed or **Cancelar** to pick a different slot.

    The system posts to `POST /recuperaciones/agendar-recuperacion` with:

    ```json
    {
      "alumnoId": "<your user ID>",
      "recuperacionId": "<ticket ID>",
      "horarioDestinoId": "<schedule ID>",
      "fechaProgramada": "<ISO date>"
    }
    ```

    A success toast **¡Clase agendada exitosamente!** confirms the booking. The page reloads with updated data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing recovery history

Click the **Mi Historial** tab to see all your past and upcoming makeup sessions. Each entry is fetched from `GET /recuperaciones/historial`.

### Recovery session statuses

| Estado | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `PROGRAMADA` | Makeup class is scheduled for a future date |
| `COMPLETADA` | You attended the makeup session |
| `CANCELADA` | The makeup was cancelled |
| `PENDIENTE` | Ticket exists but no makeup has been scheduled yet |

## Cancelling a scheduled makeup

In the **Mi Historial** tab, click the cancel button on a `PROGRAMADA` entry and confirm the prompt. The system calls `POST /recuperaciones/cancelar-recuperacion/:id`. The ticket returns to your **Pendientes** list so you can reschedule.
