The **Administrador** role gives you full control over Academia Gema's operations. Every admin page lives under `/dashboard/admin/*` and is accessible from the sidebar once you log in with an account that has the `Administrador` role.

## What the admin module covers

  <Card title="Students" icon="users" href="/admin/students">

  </Card>

  </Card>
  <Card title="Schedules" icon="calendar" href="/admin/schedules">

  <Card title="Levels" icon="trophy" href="/admin/levels">
    Define training levels (`niveles_entrenamiento`) that appear on the public schedule browser.

## Key workflows

<Steps>
  <Step title="Onboarding a new student">
    1. Go to **Students** (`/dashboard/admin/students`).
    2. Student accounts are created through the public registration flow or directly via `POST /usuarios/register` with `rol_id: "alumno"`.
    3. The student's profile includes personal data (`nombres`, `apellidos`, `email`, `telefono_personal`, `fecha_nacimiento`, `genero`), emergency contact, and medical information (`condiciones_medicas`, `seguro_medico`, `grupo_sanguineo`).
    4. Once registered, a coordinator can enroll the student in a schedule. The billing engine then generates a `cuentas_por_cobrar` record for the first billing period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Setting up a new class">
    1. Confirm the **sede** and **cancha** exist under **Locations**.
    2. Confirm the **training level** exists under **Levels**.
    3. Confirm the **coordinator** is registered under **Teachers**.
    4. Go to **Schedules** and click **Programar Clase**.
    5. Fill in `sede_id`, `cancha_id`, `coordinador_id`, `nivel_id`, `dia_semana` (1–7), `hora_inicio`, `hora_fin`, and `capacidad_max`.
    6. Submit. The system enforces the unique constraint `(cancha_id, dia_semana, hora_inicio)` — no two classes can share the same court, day, and start time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validating a student payment">
    1. Go to **Payment Validation** (`/dashboard/admin/payment-validation`).
    2. The list shows payment reports with `estado_validacion = PENDIENTE`.
    3. Open a record to view the student's uploaded voucher image (`url_comprobante`).
    4. Set the action to **APROBAR** or **RECHAZAR**, confirm the `monto_real_confirmado`, add optional `notas`, and click **Finalizar Verificación**.
    5. The system posts to `POST /pagos/validar` and updates the linked `cuentas_por_cobrar` record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All admin routes require an active session with the **Administrador** role. Unauthorized access redirects to the login page.
</Note>
